Christ the King Parish

Maranding, Lala, Lanao del Norte

About Us

Christ the King Parish serves the spiritual needs of the community in Maranding, a barangay in Lala, Lanao del Norte, Philippines. The parish is dedicated to celebrating the love and sovereignty of Christ as King.

Community Role

The parish plays an essential role in the local community, providing regular religious services, including Mass, baptisms, weddings, and other sacraments.

Church Activities

We organize various activities including catechism classes, retreats, and community service events aimed at fostering faith and community.

Feast of Christ the King

The parish celebrates the Feast of Christ the King with special Masses, processions, and community gatherings to emphasize Christ's kingship.

Community Engagement

Christ the King Parish is also involved in social and charitable activities to support the less fortunate in our community.
